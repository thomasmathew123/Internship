ERNAKULAM

Ernakulam is a vibrant and commercially significant district in Kerala, often referred to as the commercial capital of the state. It includes the bustling city of Kochi, a major port and urban center known for its historical importance, cultural diversity, and modern infrastructure. Ernakulam blends tradition with modernity, offering everything from colonial-era landmarks like Fort Kochi and Mattancherry Palace to high-rise buildings and shopping malls. The district is also a hub for education, IT, and tourism, attracting people from across the country and abroad. BEST PLACES TO VISIT


HILL PALACE MUSEUM

Hill Palace, located in Tripunithura near Kochi, Kerala, is the largest archaeological museum in the state. Built in 1865, it was once the royal administrative office and official residence of the Cochin Maharaja. Today, it houses a vast collection of royal artifacts, paintings, sculptures, and ornaments that reflect the rich heritage of Kerala's royal history. Surrounded by lush greenery and sprawling gardens, Hill Palace is not only a historical landmark but also a peaceful retreat for visitors.

MARINE DRIVE

Marine Drive in Kochi is a picturesque promenade along the backwaters, offering stunning views of the sea and the city skyline. It is a popular hangout spot for both locals and tourists, known for its peaceful atmosphere and cool breeze. The walkway features iconic structures like the Rainbow Bridge and Chinese Fishing Net Bridge. Lined with shopping malls and eateries, Marine Drive is a perfect place for evening strolls and enjoying beautiful sunsets.

WONDERLA

Wonderla is a popular amusement park located near Kochi, Kerala, known for its thrilling rides, water slides, and fun-filled attractions. It offers a mix of high-adrenaline rides, family-friendly activities, and a large water park, making it a favorite destination for all age groups. The park is well-maintained with modern facilities and ensures high safety standards. With exciting entertainment options and a vibrant atmosphere, Wonderla is the perfect spot for a fun day out with family and friends.
